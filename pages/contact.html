Bio

In 14+ years of designing with companies big and small, I’ve launched magazines, worked on Super Bowl deadlines, led teams of journalists in creating engaging online stories and helped clients reach new audiences by crafting concise and impactful websites.

From my experiences at a major metropolitan newspaper to an agency setting, I’ve leaned on a design process – research, iterations, prototypes and testing – that I believe is crucial to solving any problem.

Knowing some frontend coding helps, too. I can speak the language of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and that allows me to communicate seamlessly with development teams and deliver efficient results.

But enough about me. How can I help you? I’m available for design consultation, contract/freelance work or collaboration. Connect with me anytime right here.
